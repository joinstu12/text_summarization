[1]IMO, before you can define Accessibility you must first define disability. [2]I view that anyone should be able to access information, regardless of whether or not they have a "disability." [3]"Disability is a design flaw in the environment." [4]"People are not "disabled." [5]I like to tell people that if a blind person can't access the web page she's trying to use, the REASON is not _her_ disability, but the disability of the _web author_ who created the page.
